MESSENGERS OF TRUTH

Mission Statement

The Reflectors Community seeks to repair society by addressing the anomalies it suffers; to infuse the hope of the glory God has set for the righteous and to be an expression of the spiritual realities sealed in the name of Jesus.

  • + To make publications of all sorts that speaks hope to the reader in all forms of media.
  • + To assemble writings from the membership of The Reflectors for the publication of a periodical downloadable e-magazine.
  • + To receive and honour invites to speak at symposiums, conferences, seminars, concerts, church services and other special ceremonies.
  • + To put out short videos on every of her social media handles about her planned activities and outings.
  • + To engage questions from followers about personal crisis within the handle of the community.
